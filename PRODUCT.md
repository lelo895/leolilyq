# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Creative directors, film festival curators, clients, university evaluators on desktop + mobile. Evaluate Leo's cinematic sense, visual storytelling, technical execution.

## Product Purpose
Showcase Yongqian (Leo) Li's photography/film projects in immersive skeuomorphic portfolio. Success = memorable, tactile browsing experience highlighting organic + cinematic quality.

## Brand Personality
Tactile, nostalgic, crafted. Feel like physical scrapbook/journal on real wooden desk.

## Anti-references
- Clean, clinical, flat SaaS layout systems
- Infinite scrolling grids of identical cards
- Purely decorative geometric borders or neon-gradient tech typography

## Design Principles
1. **Physical Credibility**: Interactions/transitions rooted in physical space/mechanics (gravity, paper flex, rings, ribbons)
2. **Cinematic Framing**: Focus on immersive presentations of visual media
3. **Restrained Skeuomorphism**: Maintain layout readability + performance. Skeuomorphic details delight, not impede user flow

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` (bypass high-velocity notebook tilts/transitions)
- Standard high-contrast ratios for text on paper surfaces
