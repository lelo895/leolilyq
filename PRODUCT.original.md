# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Creative directors, film festival curators, clients looking for photography/videography services, and university evaluators. They are viewing this on desktop and mobile devices, looking to evaluate Leo's cinematic sense, visual storytelling ability, and technical execution.

## Product Purpose
To showcase Yongqian (Leo) Li's photography and film projects in a unique, highly immersive, skeuomorphic portfolio interface. Success is a memorable, tactile browsing experience that highlights the organic and cinematic quality of Leo's work.

## Brand Personality
Tactile, nostalgic, crafted. The experience should feel like flipping through a physical scrapbook/journal on a real wooden desk.

## Anti-references
- Clean, clinical, flat SaaS layout systems.
- Infinite scrolling grids of identical cards.
- Purely decorative geometric borders or neon-gradient tech typography.

## Design Principles
1. **Physical Credibility**: Interactions and transitions should feel rooted in physical space and mechanics (gravity, paper flex, binding rings, ribbons).
2. **Cinematic Framing**: Focus on immersive presentations of visual media, matching the photography and film medium.
3. **Restrained Skeuomorphism**: Maintain layout readability and performance; skeuomorphic details should delight rather than impede user flow.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Respect user preference for reduced motion (`prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`) by bypassing high-velocity notebook tilts and transitions.
- Standard high-contrast ratios for text elements against paper surfaces.
